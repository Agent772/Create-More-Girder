## Notes

- **Andesite vs. Industrial Iron** — nearly identical dark gray range; andesite has greenish highlights (`#9AA49D`, `#B4C1B8`) while industrial iron stays neutral (`#989797`, `#BCBCBC`).
- **Vanilla Create girder vs. Industrial Iron** — palettes overlap almost completely, confirming industrial iron as the closest material match.
- **Copper oxidation gradient** — warm orange (copper) → mixed green-orange (exposed) → mossy green (weathered) → teal (oxidized).
- **Shared outline colors** — most girders use `#000000` and `#030303` for outlines/shadows.
